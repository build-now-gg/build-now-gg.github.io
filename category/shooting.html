    Shooting games are a genre of video games that center around combat using firearms or ranged weapons, emphasizing accuracy, reflexes, and strategy. These games can be played from a first-person perspective (FPS), like Call of Duty and Halo, where the player experiences the action through the eyes of the character, or from a third-person perspective, like Fortnite or Gears of War, offering a wider view of the environment. Shooting games often involve fast-paced action, a variety of weapons, and tactical gameplay, with players battling enemies in solo campaigns, competitive multiplayer modes, or cooperative missions. The genre is known for its adrenaline-fueled combat, immersive environments, and a focus on skillful aiming and strategic movement.
