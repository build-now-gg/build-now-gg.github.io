    What is Burrito Bison?

    Burrito Bison is a wildly popular mobile and web game series developed by Juicy Beast. The game combines elements of physics-based gameplay, strategy, and humor, making it a favorite among casual gamers. The premise of the game revolves around a luchador (wrestler) named Burrito Bison, who is on a mission to reclaim his stolen recipes from the evil Candy Land.

    With its vibrant graphics, quirky characters, and addictive gameplay, Burrito Bison has garnered a massive fan base since its initial release. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the series, this guide will provide you with everything you need to know about Burrito Bison.

    Gameplay Overview

    The gameplay of Burrito Bison is simple yet highly engaging. Players launch Burrito Bison into the air, aiming to cover as much distance as possible while collecting gummies and defeating enemies. The game features a unique blend of physics-based mechanics and strategic upgrades, allowing players to improve their performance with each run.

    Here are some key aspects of the gameplay:

    • Launching: Use the slingshot to launch Burrito Bison as far as possible. Timing and angle are crucial for maximizing distance.
    • Gummies: Collect gummies to earn currency, which can be used to purchase upgrades and power-ups.
    • Enemies: Defeat enemies like the Evil Elves and Gummy Bears to earn extra points and gummies.
    • Upgrades: Spend gummies on upgrades to enhance Burrito Bison's abilities, such as increased launch power, better bounce, and more.
